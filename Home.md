**.NET MAUI** is:

* Multi-platform native UI
* Deploy to multiple devices across mobile & desktop
* Using a single project, single codebase
* Evolution of [Xamarin.Forms](https://github.com/xamarin/xamarin.forms)
* Shipping with .NET 6
